Driving Laws

  • Stay on the right side of the road.
  • Stay below the speed limit or go the speed limit.
  • Stop at stop signs.
  • Pay attention to lights.
  • Do not drink and drive.
  • Tickets vary in cost, depending on the violation.
  • If you are high, do not drive.
  • Seat belts must be worn unless you are travelling less than thirty meters.
  • If an emergency or military vehicle is passing, pull over.
  • You must have a driver's license to drive in Mineia.
  • You must be at least 8 years old to acquire a license.
  • If you fail the driving test 5 times, you must wait 2 years to try again.
  • If you fail the driving test, you must wait two months before you may attempt again.
  • No screens while driving unless it is a mounted map system.
  • Only a registered trainer may give a driving test.
  • If you fail the driving test 7 times you may not attempt to acquire a license again.
  • You can only drive a registered vehicle in Mineia.
  • You must register a vehicle within a month of purchase.
  • Military or Emergency vehicles have right of way.
  • If you are in a crash or roll, report it immediately.
  • No lane splitting on a vehicle with more than two wheels.
  • If you have turn signals, use them or remove them.
  • Yield to larger vehicles.
  • If you are the passenger in a crash you can sue the driver for damages if they occur.
  • If you are towing more than 750kgs you must have a sign telling others you can't break well.
  • If you crash into someone you must help them and make sure that they are okay.
  • If you injure someone in a crash you must pay compensation to them.
  • If you crash into someone you may be jailed depending on the damage.
  • If you crash into someone you will have your license suspended for the time of one month.
  • If you crash into someone more than five times you will have your license revoked.
  • If you do not use your blinker at intersections you may be fined.
  • If you go 10kph below the speed limit you will be fined.
  • If you get 3 tickets in 6 months you will have your license suspended.
  • You may not drive a vehicle or pull a trailer if the frame is very rusty or bent.
  • At night if you have working headlights you must use them.
  • You may not have more passengers in a vehicle than seats.
  • If you hit an object on the side of the road you are responsible for all damages.
  • If you are transporting animals you must put a sign on your vehicle.
  • If you are losing control of your vehicle and you see a vehicle transporting animals, do all in your power to not hit the vehicle.
  • If you see a vehicle on the side of the road on its own you have to stop and help them or be fined.
  • If a vehicle in front of you has a heavy load sign, stay 7.5 meters back.
  • You are always responsible if you hit a vehicle transporting animals.
  • If there is an object in the road that you can move, move it otherwise report it.
  • If you see an unidentified military vehicle, report it.
  • If there are geese on the road, keep driving just slow down or be fined; they will move.
  • Pay attention to the road at all times.
  • You may not leave your vehicle running unattended.
  • If you try to steal a vehicle, face the firing squad.
  • Do not leave your keys in your vehicle.
  • If you have a flat tire stop driving and have it towed.

Building Laws

  • You must have house insurance if you wish to buy a house.
  • If you apply for a mortgage, you must pay your house back in 12 months.
  • If you apply for a mortgage, you can pay it back in 6 months.
  • If you apply for a mortgage and don’t pay it back within the 12 or 6 month period, you will be fined.
  • If you don’t pay your mortgage on time and you are fined you may have your license suspended until you are able to pay it back.
  • If you are suspended, the government will take away your car until you can pay your mortgage in full.
  • If you wish to build a house, the house cost must be less than half of your bank account or you must get a loan.
  • No building shall exceed 10 stories in height unless it includes at least one floor dedicated to public green space. Rooftop gardens or parks must be integrated into all buildings exceeding 6 stories.
  • All new buildings must include windows that allow natural sunlight to reach the furthest corners of the interior space.
  • Construction work is prohibited between the hours of 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM in all residential areas.
  • All buildings must use locally sourced materials or eco-friendly alternatives. Buildings constructed from materials that do not align with environmental sustainability guidelines will be fined.
  • All buildings must be designed with future adaptability in mind. At least 30% of the building’s structure must be modular and easily reconfigurable to accommodate future needs.

Permit Laws

  • You must get a permit to enter unauthorized areas unless you are highly regarded in the Military and Government.
  • If you have a fake permit you get a fine.
  • If you are a civilian you must get a permit from the king or a general to enter the bunker.
  • If you want to cut down a tree with more than a 3-inch diameter you must get a logging permit.

Election Laws

  • You may not spread false information about a candidate.
  • You may not have a political party if you are caught with one; you will be pulled from the election and fined.
